Keith's Lisbon Birthday Adventure

Welcome to the Birthday Adventure App!


This app contains a list of activites designed to be done in order, but if you want to skip ahead, go for it.

Each activity is presented in two parts: The Adventure and The Reveal.

The Adventure displays GPS coordinates and directions from the previous location. When you think you've reached your mystery destination, tap Reveal.

The Reveal contains the name, address, and maps link to your destination, plus Monica color commentary.

The entire set of adventures spans lunch, afternoon exploring, dinner, and after-dinner outings. No pressure to do it all, they're just suggestions.

Happy Birthday!
